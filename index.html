Are you currently tracking student's attendance in a Microsoft Excel Workbook or even worse manually with pen and paper?

Every school year, public and private institutions have to ensure children are enrolled in a school system. Throughout the school year the staff have to provide attendace records and account for the students. Unfortunately, a student may not attend school on a regular basis and this must be tracked accordingly. The goal is to have a 100% attendance rate but that is not realistic. With our services, the institutions will be able to easily maintain their attendace records and ultimately increase their respective attendance rate by identify a correlation between the decline in a student's attendace and their behavior or home-life and provide assistance where needed.

Advance Attendance Tracking System is a company that provides a premier consulting service and a modernized application to allow our clients to effectively track attendance. Our consulting services are innovative, comprehensive, and effective.
